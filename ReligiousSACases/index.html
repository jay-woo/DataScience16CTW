Data Science - Spring 2016
Casey Alvarado and Jay Woo
A History of Abuse
As of 2002, 4,392 clerics were "credibly" and "not implausibly" accused of sexually assaulting 10,667 minors.
Slap On the Wrists
Of the accused, only 55% of them received some form of legal punishment.
Gender Bias
The proportion of clerics who did receive legal punishment was highest for nuns.
Data By State
The states with the most accused priests are California, Massachusetts, and New York.
Closeknit Catholic Communities
There is a strong correlation between the percentage of Catholics and the number of priests who were accused in each state.
How large is the scandal today?
As of 2013, the number of accused priests has grown to 6,429, and the number of survivors has grown to 17,259.
What's next?
After being publicly exposed by the media, the Vatican has admitted to making "enormous mistakes."

But the church must clearly do more to prevent these numbers from growing.
